Game Description

Welcome to Matching Mystery – Dragonland, where your journey begins in an enchanted garden beside a castle brimming with magic and mystery. This game blends the satisfying strategy of match-3 puzzles with the creative depth of merge gameplay. Complete thrilling tile challenges, earn magical chests, and use those items to revive a forgotten land — one flower, building, and dragon at a time.
As you explore Dragonland, you’ll uncover mythical dragons, uncover secrets of the castle, and decorate your garden in your unique style. From barren land to a blooming paradise — you are the creator.
Whether you love puzzles, collecting cute creatures, or building magical kingdoms, this is your world to shape
