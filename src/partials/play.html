How to Play

  • 1

    Start in the Garden

    Begin with a basic garden and castle grounds

  • 2

    Solve Match-3 Levels

    Complete puzzles to earn magical item chests

  • 3

    Merge Items

    Combine similar items to evolve them into powerful objects or structures

  • 4

    Decorate and Unlock

    Use your merged items to beautify and expand your kingdom

  • 5

    Collect Dragons

    Hatch baby dragons and evolve them into majestic magical allies

  • 6

    Explore Realms

    Unlock new islands with unique styles and resources

  • 7

    Compete in Events

    Join timed events to earn limited-time rewards and special dragons
