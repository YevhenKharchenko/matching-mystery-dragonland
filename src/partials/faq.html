FAQ

  • Is Matching Mystery – Dragonland free to play?

    Yes! The game is free to download with optional in-app purchases

  • Can I play offline?

    Absolutely. The game supports offline mode so you can play anywhere

  • What kind of dragons can I unlock?

    From baby fire dragons to majestic crystal wyverns – there’s a huge collection to discover and raise

  • Are there new updates?

    Yes! We regularly release new puzzles, dragons, events, and islands

  • Can I customize my island?

    Totally! You can decorate with buildings, flowers, dragons, and rare treasures
