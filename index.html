Merge, Match, and Enchant Your Garden!

Game Description

Welcome to Matching Mystery – Dragonland, where your journey begins in an enchanted garden beside a castle brimming with magic and mystery. This game blends the satisfying strategy of match-3 puzzles with the creative depth of merge gameplay. Complete thrilling tile challenges, earn magical chests, and use those items to revive a forgotten land — one flower, building, and dragon at a time.
As you explore Dragonland, you’ll uncover mythical dragons, uncover secrets of the castle, and decorate your garden in your unique style. From barren land to a blooming paradise — you are the creator.
Whether you love puzzles, collecting cute creatures, or building magical kingdoms, this is your world to shape

Main Gameplay Features

  • Match-3 Adventures

    Solve colorful and dynamic puzzles with boosters and unique tile mechanics

    Daily & Weekly Events

    Participate in themed garden events and unlock seasonal dragons and decorations

  • Merge Magic System

    Merge collected items to evolve them into rare resources, buildings, and adorable dragons

    Smooth Controls

    Swipe, match, and merge with intuitive controls and responsive gameplay

  • Island Customization

    Build and decorate your island with magical structures, flowers, and scenery

    Offline Mode

    Play offline anytime, perfect for mobile gaming on the go

  • Chest Rewards

    Win magical chests in match-3 levels to collect new items for merging

    Enchanted Storyline

    Meet characters, uncover secrets, and restore the castle to its former glory.

How to Play

  • 4
